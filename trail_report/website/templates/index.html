About

How do we determine trail conditions?

  • Collect Trail Information

    All hike information and trail reports collected from the WTA site.

  • Add Weather Trends

    Current and past weather trends for each location collected from NOAA.

  • Fill In The Gaps

    Using trail location, elevation, and hiking date, similar trail reports from the past are found to give us insight.

  • Determine Current Conditions

    Using the collected data, four individual models were built- predicting snow, trail, road, and bug conditions.

    go hike well
    informed!
